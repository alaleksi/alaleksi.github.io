Creations for Players

Armor Modification: Spiked Armor (5th edition Dungeons & Dragons))

Cost: +75 gp
Weight: +5 lbs
Restrictions: Not usable with padded, leather, or hide armor
Stealth: Disadvantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks (unless already imposed by the armor)
Features:
Spiked Strikes: While wearing this armor, you can make unarmed strikes with it, dealing 1d4 + Str piercing damage.
Damage Scaling:
With Tavern Brawler: Damage becomes 1d6 + Str
With Unarmed Fighting Style: Damage becomes 1d8 + Str
Bonus Action Attack: Once per turn, you can make one attack with the spikes as a Bonus Action, dealing the same damage as your spiked strike.
Grapple Damage (Unarmed Fighting Style only): At the start of your turn, deal 1d4 + Str piercing damage to one creature you're grappling, in addition to the normal 1d4 bludgeoning.
Ideal For: Fighters, Barbarians, and brawler builds who want their armor to hit back.
Note for DMs: Consider attunement or magical upgrades for high-level use.
